Abstract

World university rankings , updated annually, can not only reveal the global educational situations, but also give reference for students to select university that fit the most for them. Different institutions have developed various kinds of methodologies to rank global universities. Some specific univer- sities can be ranked top in one ranking system but performs worse in another, which brings confusions for people to decide. Therefore in this project we plan to visualize global university rankings based on U.S. News and QS (Quacquarelli Symonds) methodologies. We firstly visualize these two rankings separately, and then give comparison to help people evaluate. Then, we present visualizations to explore QS rankings in country level, trying to seek relationships between national education levels with Gross Domestic Product (GDP) per capita and world population by countries. We hope to include novel aspects in this project to give deep analysis for the World University Rankings.
